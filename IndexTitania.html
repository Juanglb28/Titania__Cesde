The Menu

Rogue’s menu is a culinary and mixology marvel.
The drinks are crafted by master mixologists who
take inspiration from the mutant abilities of the X
Men characters. Signature cocktails include
“Mystique’s Mimicry,” a chameleon-like drink that
changes color and flavor as you consume it,
and “Cable,” a potent concoction.

The Experience

What sets Titania apart from other
bars is the experience it offers.
Regular themed nights, exclusive
events, and guest appearances by
renowned mixologists and chefs
keep the excitement alive. Themed
nights might include “Mutant Mondays”
with trivia and cosplay, or
“Telepathic Thursdays”
with live jazz
and psychic readings.
